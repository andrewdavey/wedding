Wedding Day Schedule (Saturday 18th)

12:00 Arrival at Heartlands

Heartlands is a visitor attraction and World Heritage Site in Cornwall, built around an old Engine House. We're getting married in the Film Room, a converted space inside one of the old mining buildings. It's part of the exhibition rooms, next to the visitor centre.
